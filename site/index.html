Here is the math.

Four lines. That is the whole product.

pace    to_spend = week_left ÷ 5h_windows_left  What to spend this window.
anchor  cap = burned ÷ used%  Pinned to /usage.
burn    per line = (input + 5·output + 1.25·cache_write + 0.1·cache_read) × model_price  Different models cost different
models  haiku ⅓ · sonnet 1 · opus 5⁄3 · fable 10⁄3  From Anthropic's price sheet, daily.

A Fable token costs 10× a Haiku token. maxx counts what they cost.

How to read the bar

Two rails: this session, and the week.

session     ·

 

week       318,402k left · +24,013k banked · 6d

Fill = what's left. ╎ = pace. Past it: banked. Short: too fast.

  • session: safe to spend right now.
  • week: what's left of your 7 days, live.
  • context: how full, plus model, branch, spend, cache.

Install

One line in your terminal. Restart Claude Code. Done.

$ curl -fsSL https://meetmaxx.co/install | bash

Then /maxx for your stats. /maxx session for this window.
